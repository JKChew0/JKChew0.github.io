Projects

Euromillions number generator

My first project is a lottery ticket generator for the Euromillions. It is similar to the lucky dip option where numbers are selected at random however, the program provides a little more control over which numbers get chosen.

For example, you have the option of selecting how many high or low numbers you would like. That is, numbers from 1-25 (low), and from 26-50(high). Furthermore, you can select how many even or odd numbers you would like.

About

My name is Joshua Chew. I am originally from Australia and moved to the UK at nineteen to study Electronic Engineering at the University of Portsmouth. This is where I was first introduced to programming in the form of BASIC and C and discovered a love for it. I taught myself the basics of HTML, CSS, and Javascript during my spare time.

I have since taught myself Python using the book 'Python Crash Course - A hands-on, project-based introduction to programming' by Eric Matthes.
